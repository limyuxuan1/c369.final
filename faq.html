Frequently Asked Questions


INTCash is a service that allows you to send and receive money quickly and securely, either domestically or internationally.
You can create an account by visiting the INTCash website, then following the registration process which usually involves providing personal information.
Fees vary depending on the type of transfer, amount, and destination. Check the fee schedule on the INTCash website or app for detailed information.
Transaction limits can vary based on account type, verification status, and destination. Refer to the INTCash website or contact customer support for specific limits applicable to your account.
Transfer times depend on the method used and the destination. Transfers can be instant or take a few business days. Check the estimated delivery times for your specific transfer.
You can track your transfer using the tracking feature in the INTCash app or website by entering the transaction reference number.
